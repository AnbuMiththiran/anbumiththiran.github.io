Is English the  knowledge?

“No, English is a language that is a tool for communication.”Everyone said.

But, many companies are looking their workers should spoken in English as so well. In their point of view, knowledge is secondary. English speaking skill is primary.

I was born in Tamil Nadu, India. We are speaking as well in Tamil. Tamil is my mother tongue. If I need to get a job after my degree completion, I should learn English and speak in English as well. Hindi is the majority states communication language in India. In Tamil Nadu, English has the first priority in industrial and technological field.

Under the English, we can communicate around over the world. So, learning English leads us to communicate more people, read more books and gain more knowledge.

“Okay, English is a language that I learnt. I scare to speak in English about if anything I speak wrongly. How to overcome this problem?”Everyone asked.

I think that practice will made them perfectly. So, I started to write in English, speak in English for exercises. I hope that it will help me to perfection in English language communication. Because, I am looking for a job in outside from my native places. I want to become a successful person in the world. I have to work hardly for it.

I read English newspapers daily. I interested to invest in stock Market. So, I read lot of thinks about the stock market. I have passed a bechlor degree in Electronics and Communication engineering. So, I have to develop my knowledge in the field of my qualification.

I have to learn about stress management. Because, my stress made me to unaware in things happening around me. It also leads to magnify my problem in communication with other people. But, I didn ’t lost my hope. I will overcome my problems and will become enough to be successful in my life.
