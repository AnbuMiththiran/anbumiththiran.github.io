About Me

Who I Am

I am Anbudan Miththiran — a writer, thinker, podcaster, and storyteller. A devotee of Lord Krishna and Lord Shiva, I walk the path of wisdom, love, and social reform.

Born into an economically poorest family, my native village lies near Puliangudi, Tamil Nadu. From childhood, my heart searched for the purest form of love — a search that eventually shaped me into a writer.

I believe words can heal, awaken, and transform society.
